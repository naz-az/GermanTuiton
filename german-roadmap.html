Language Course: German

Roadmap

German Course Pathway

  • A1.1

    A1.1 is described as the “Minimal assessable level” of language according to the CEFR. It is a 6-month course that meets once a week.

  • A1.2

    A1.2 is described as the “2nd phase” of the A1 level. It is also a 6-month course, but it includes an additional month of intensive preparation for the A1 exam.

  • A1 Exam

    The A1 exam is a certified language test that places students in the “Basic User” category. Learners at this level can understand sentences, communicate in simple and routine tasks, and describe in simple terms their immediate environment or needs. Approximately it takes between 80 and 200 hours of study to reach this level.
